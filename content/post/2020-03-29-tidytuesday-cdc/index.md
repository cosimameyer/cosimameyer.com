This week's [TidyTuesday](https://github.com/rfordatascience/tidytuesday) was all about traumatic brain injuries with data by the [CDC](https://www.cdc.gov/traumaticbraininjury/). [Dennis Hammerschmidt](https://dennis-hammerschmidt.netlify.com/) and I looked at how different levels of severity in traumatic brain injuries change over time and across military services. We adjusted the theme so that it fits CDC's color scheme and animated our graphs using [gganimate](https://gganimate.com/articles/gganimate.html).
