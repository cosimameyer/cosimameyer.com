This is a late version of a [TidyTuesday](https://github.com/rfordatascience/tidytuesday). I started working on it a while ago and could not finish it -- but here you go! The TidyTuesday was about Broadway musicals with a dataset by [Playbill](https://www.playbill.com/grosses). I plotted an arc diagram with [ggraph](https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/ggraph/index.html) visualizing how long the musical was played, coloring the top 6 musicals based on the weekly gross the plays generated. I colored those six musicals with colors from their original logo.

While it might not come as a surprise to you, "The Phantom of the Opera" appears to be the most popular musical -- both in terms of length and weekly gross!
