This blog post kicks off our new blog [**Methods Bites**](https://www.mzes.uni-mannheim.de/socialsciencedatalab/)! 

The [blog post](https://www.mzes.uni-mannheim.de/socialsciencedatalab/article/datavis/) by Richard Traunmüller and me provides illustrative examples from Richard Traunmüller’s one-day workshop in the MZES Social Science Data Lab in Fall 2016. It walks you through several ways of visualizing your data effectively and gives you some neat code lines that are ready to (re)use for your own projects!
