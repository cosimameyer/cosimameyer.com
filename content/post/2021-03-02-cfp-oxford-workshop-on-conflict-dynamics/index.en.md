The CfP for the 5th edition of our Workshop on Conflict Dynamics is online! Following the great success of our first virtual setting, we decided to stay online this year. If you are a young scholar in the field of conflict studies, submit your abstract [here](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/conflict-dynamics/workshop-2021/call-for-papers/) -- we are very much looking forward to many inspiring submissions!
