 [WUMAN](http://www.wuman.de) is the network for female researchers at the University of Mannheim. As part of their initiative to give women in science a voice, they conducted a series of interviews. As part of this series, I also got the chance to tell more about my own research, my passion, and my involvement with [Rationale Altruisten Mannheim e.V.](https://ram-ev.de).

 If you want to know more about this, read my short [interview with WUMAN here](https://wuman307139351.wordpress.com/2019/12/03/cosima-meyer-universitaet-mannheim/).
