This [blog post](https://www.mzes.uni-mannheim.de/socialsciencedatalab/article/efficient-data-r/) (together with Denis Cohen, Marcel Neunhoeffer, and Oliver Rittmann) is all about efficient data management! It gives you a quick intro into how to set up a possible workflow in R and continues with a comparison between base R and the tidyverse, and shows you how to write more complex functions in R.
