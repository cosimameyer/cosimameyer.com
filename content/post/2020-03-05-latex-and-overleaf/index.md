Do you fancy learning LaTeX? LaTeX is a high quality typesetting system that that facilitates the production of well-formatted document. It has become highly popular in academic settings as an alternative to common typewriting systems (e.g., Word). [Dennis Hammerschmidt](https://dennis-hammerschmidt.netlify.com/) and I wrote a hands-on tutorial that shows you how to use [LaTeX with Overleaf](https://www.mzes.uni-mannheim.de/socialsciencedatalab/article/latex-intro/) and is now published on [MZES Methods Bites](https://www.mzes.uni-mannheim.de/socialsciencedatalab/). 

We also introduce you to a template for scientific papers (you can access it in the [Overleaf templates gallery](https://www.overleaf.com/latex/templates/template-term-paper/jbymjwgdbzkp)). It comes with a nice folder structure that allows a good overview of the different text parts.
