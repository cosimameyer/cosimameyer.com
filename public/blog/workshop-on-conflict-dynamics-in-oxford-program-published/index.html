As one of the organizers, I am incredibly excited to share this year’s program for our annual Workshop on Conflict Dynamics (virtual) at Oxford University!

We have fascinating panels with excellent speakers, exciting round tables on topics such as “communicating your research (beyond academia)” and “social media”, as well as excellent discussants! To benefit the most from the network that we have built during the past 3+ years, we organize our first “Pass it on” session where experts get together to share their knowledge with others.
